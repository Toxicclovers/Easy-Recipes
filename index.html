  • 1 Pound of Beef Mince
  • Half a White Onion
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons of Garlic and Herb Tomato Paste
  • Sprinkle of Low-fat Grated Cheese
  • Low Calorie Tortillas
  • (Optional) Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Chilli Powder

Step 1: Take half the onion and chop it up into small pieces (about the size of Tic Tacs), then put it to the side.

Step 2: Get the salt, pepper, and any other seasonings you've chosen, and set them aside.

Step 3: Preheat a pan on medium heat for 2 minutes.

Step 4: Put the diced onion into the pan and cook until it begins to show a little bit of color.

Step 5: Add the beef mince to the pan and mix it around until it covers the pan and the onions are mixed in well.

Step 6: Continue mixing until the mince is about 70% brown and 30% red.

Step 7: Add salt, pepper, and a decent sprinkle of low-fat cheese until it covers 50% of the pan. Add any optional seasonings now too.

Step 8: Continue mixing until all the red is gone and the cheese is melted.

Step 9: Remove the pan from the element and get your tortillas ready.

Step 10: Put a decent spoonful of the mince onto each tortilla and sprinkle a little more cheese on top. Then roll it up and close it (look online if unsure how to close a burrito).

Final Step: Toast the burrito on the pan until both sides are nice and brown, then eat it or store it in the fridge until ready to eat.

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • Shredded Cheese (of your choice)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Shredded Ham
  • Butter

Step 1: Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk with a fork until fully mixed.

Step 2: Lightly butter the pan on low heat until covered.

Step 3: Pour the eggs into the pan and spread evenly.

Step 4: Add sprinkles of cheese across the pan.

Step 5: Sprinkle shredded ham until you're happy with the amount.

Step 6: Put a lid on the pan and raise the heat to medium.

Step 7: Check every minute until the top of the egg is fully cooked to your liking.

Step 8: Slide the egg pizza carefully onto a plate (or eat directly from the pan once it cools).

Final Step: (Optional) Add your favorite hot sauce or Kewpie mayo on top and enjoy!
